How this estimate works

This calculator uses simple compound interest as an approximation: it takes the number of full years between your issue date and today, applies the current annual rate for that series, and compounds it once per year.

Real Treasury bonds compound semi-annually and I bonds have a rate that changes every 6 months, so your actual bond's real value (found in the Savings Bond Calculator on TreasuryDirect.gov) may differ slightly from this estimate.
