Privacy Impact Assessments are conducted to evaluate how personal information is collected, used, stored, and protected within our systems. These assessments help identify privacy risks and ensure appropriate safeguards are maintained.

Depending on the services provided, we may request information necessary to verify identity, process transactions, maintain account records, and communicate with account holders.

  • Mailing address

  • Telephone number
  • Government-issued identification information
  • Bank account information where applicable

2. Protection of Personal Information

Administrative, technical, and physical safeguards are used to help protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

3. Cookies and Website Usage

Session cookies and standard website analytics may be used to improve performance, security, and user experience. Session cookies are removed automatically when the browser is closed.

4. Security

Secure communication technologies are used where appropriate to protect information transmitted between your browser and our systems.

5. Contact

Questions regarding privacy practices or requests relating to personal information may be submitted through the Contact Us page.
